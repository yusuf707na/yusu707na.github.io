Frequently Asked Questions - SpeedTest Pro

What is SpeedTest Pro?

SpeedTest Pro is a mobile app designed to test your internet speed, helping you monitor upload and download rates, latency, and overall connection quality.

How do I start a speed test?

Open the app and press the "Start Test" button. SpeedTest Pro will automatically analyze your network and display your connection results within seconds.

Can I track my past speed tests?

Yes, SpeedTest Pro logs your previous speed tests so you can easily review your connection history and monitor any changes over time.

What networks can SpeedTest Pro test?

Our app works with all network types, including Wi-Fi, 3G, 4G, 5G, and Ethernet connections. You can test speeds across any network your device is connected to.

Why are my test results different each time?

Network speeds can vary due to factors like network congestion, distance from the router, and device limitations. Running multiple tests provides a more accurate average speed.

How do I interpret my speed test results?

Results show your download speed (receiving data), upload speed (sending data), and ping (response time). Higher download/upload speeds and lower ping times indicate a better connection.

Is there a limit to how many tests I can run?

There are no limits! SpeedTest Pro lets you perform unlimited tests so you can check your network whenever you need to.

Does SpeedTest Pro use my data?

Running a test uses a small amount of data to measure speeds. If you’re on a limited plan, it’s recommended to use Wi-Fi or be mindful of data usage during testing.

Can I share my speed test results?

Yes! SpeedTest Pro provides an easy share feature to let you share your results directly from the app.
